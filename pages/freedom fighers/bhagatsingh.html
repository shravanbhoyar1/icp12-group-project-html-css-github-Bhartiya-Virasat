About the Legend

Bhagat Singh was an Indian revolutionary who played a pivotal role in the freedom struggle against British rule. Known for his fearless acts and sacrifice at just 23, he remains a symbol of courage and patriotism.

About the Legend

Bhagat Singh was an Indian revolutionary who played a pivotal role in the freedom struggle against British rule. Known for his fearless acts and sacrifice at just 23, he remains a symbol of courage and patriotism.

His Journey

1928

Joined Hindustan Socialist Republican Association (HSRA).

1929

Protested British rule by throwing bombs in the Central Assembly.

1931

Executed at the age of 23, igniting a revolutionary spirit across India.
